About Vault 96 Co.

Vault 96 Co. is centered around handmade accessories, custom creations, and handpainted products.

From friendship bracelets and necklaces to personalized glasses and other fun pieces, the goal is to create items that feel creative, personal, and unique.

Each product is made with care and designed to feel special, whether it’s a gift, a custom request, or something made just for everyday fun.
